VISION

Saint Micheal College of Caraga Envision to be a University by 2035 and Upholds Spiritual Formation and Excellence in Teaching, Service, and Research. 🏫

Come be a Michealinian!!! 🤗

MISSION

  • SMCC shall provide spitual formation and learning culture that will ensure the students with excellent and rewarding learning experience that transform lives, abound spirituality, develop skills and prepare future leader.
  • SMCC shall engage in dyanamic, innovative, and interdisciplinary research to advance and achieve institutional initiatives.
  • SMCC shall commit to serve the diverse and local communities by fostering innovations through service-learning that enhances reciprocal community partnerships for spiritual and social development.

GOALS

Uphold Culture of Excellence in the Areas of Spiritual Formation, Instruction, Research, and Extension, thus produce Graduates that are Globally Competent, Spiritually Embodied, and Socially Responsible.

HTML VS XHTML

Below is a simple comparison of HTML and XHTML syntax:

<!-- HTML version (not self-closed) -->
                            <img src="images/profile.jpg" alt="Profile image">
    
                            <!-- XHTML-style version (self-closed, lowercase, quoted attributes) -->
                            <img src="images/profile.jpg" alt="Profile image" />

XHTML is stricter and requires properly closed tags, lowercase names, and quoted attributes.
